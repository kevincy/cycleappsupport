Terms & Wellness Disclaimer

Effective date: June 29, 2026

1. Purpose of MiraCycle

MiraCycle is a wellness and personal record-keeping app for cycle tracking, daily logs, local insights, and PDF summaries. It is intended to help users organize their own information.

2. Not Medical Advice

MiraCycle does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment, disease prevention, contraception, fertility guarantees, or emergency medical services. Predictions and insights are estimates based on logged information and should not be used as the sole basis for medical, contraception, pregnancy, or fertility decisions.

3. Consult a Professional

Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical concerns, unusual symptoms, severe pain, pregnancy questions, contraception, fertility planning, or any health decision.

4. User Responsibility

You are responsible for reviewing your entries, export settings, and shared reports. Before sending a PDF report, confirm that it includes only the information you intend to share.

5. Availability and Changes

MiraCycle features may change over time. Support documents may be updated to reflect app updates, App Store requirements, or privacy improvements.

6. Contact

For support, documentation corrections, or privacy questions, open an issue at github.com/kevincy/cycleappsupport/issues.
