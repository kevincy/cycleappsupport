User Guide

Last updated: June 29, 2026

MiraCycle is a private cycle journal for calendar-based cycle tracking, daily symptom logs, local insights, and doctor-ready PDF reports.

1. Getting Started

  1. Open MiraCycle.
  2. Complete onboarding by selecting the dates from your most recent period.
  3. MiraCycle uses those calendar dates to estimate your current cycle timing.
  4. You can update period dates later from the Calendar tab.

2. Calendar-Based Logging

The Calendar tab is the main place to record cycle information.

  1. Open the Calendar tab.
  2. Tap a date.
  3. Choose whether your period started, ended, or occurred on that date.
  4. Add optional details such as flow, pain level, symptoms, mood, discharge, sleep, body metrics, sex/protection, and private notes.
  5. Save the entry.

3. Voice-Assisted Entry

MiraCycle can help turn spoken notes into a draft log. For example, you can say: “Period started today, light flow, cramps six out of ten, felt anxious, slept poorly.”

  1. Tap the voice input button in the logging sheet.
  2. Allow microphone and speech recognition access when prompted.
  3. Speak your cycle note.
  4. Review the draft fields.
  5. Confirm before saving.

Voice entry is designed to reduce typing. You should always review the draft before saving.

4. Insights

The Insights tab summarizes patterns based on your saved calendar logs. It can help you notice timing shifts, symptom clusters, pain patterns, and recurring mood or sleep changes.

5. PDF Doctor Report

MiraCycle can generate a local PDF summary for appointments. The report can include recent calendar logs, actual period history, cycle timing, and selected details.

File names use this format:

MiraCycle_Report_YYYYMMDD-YYYYMMDD_YYYYMMDD_HHMMSS.pdf

6. Export Privacy Controls

Before sharing a PDF report, use the export switches in Insights to choose what appears in the report. You can include or exclude period history, flow, pain, symptoms, mood, discharge, sleep, sex/protection, body metrics, and private notes.

Private notes, sex/protection, and body metrics are sensitive. Review export settings before sharing with anyone.

7. Deleting or Resetting Data

If available in your version, open Privacy or Settings and choose the option to delete local data. This removes saved profile information, logs, period dates, and privacy settings from the device.

8. Troubleshooting

My prediction looks wrong

Update your actual period dates in the Calendar tab. Predictions improve when your logged dates reflect your real cycle history.

Voice input is unavailable

Check iOS Settings and make sure MiraCycle has microphone and speech recognition permissions.

I cannot find the exported PDF

Use the iOS share sheet to save the PDF to Files, send it by email, or share it with a clinician. The PDF is generated locally when you share it.

9. Important Wellness Notice

MiraCycle is for personal wellness tracking and record keeping. It does not diagnose, treat, prevent disease, provide contraception, or guarantee fertility predictions. Consult a qualified healthcare professional before making medical decisions.
