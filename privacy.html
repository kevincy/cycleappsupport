Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 3, 2026

MiraCycle is designed as a local-first cycle tracking and wellness journaling app. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app may store, how it is used, what permissions are requested, and what privacy choices you have.

1. Summary

  • MiraCycle does not require an account for core tracking.
  • Cycle logs are stored locally on your device.
  • The developer does not collect your cycle logs through an app server in the current version.
  • The app does not include third-party advertising SDKs or third-party analytics SDKs in the current version.
  • PDF reports are generated locally from your saved calendar logs.
  • You choose which sensitive fields appear in exported PDF reports.
  • MiraCycle is not designed to sell personal health data.

2. Information You May Enter

You may choose to record information such as:

  • Period dates, period start, period end, predicted cycle timing, and flow level.
  • Pain intensity, symptoms, mood, sleep, discharge, and body metrics.
  • Sex/protection fields if you choose to record them.
  • Private notes if you choose to write them.
  • Voice-assisted transcript text before you confirm a log.
  • Privacy preferences such as app lock, quick hide, export choices, and hidden sensitive fields.

3. How Information Is Used

MiraCycle uses your saved entries to provide core app features, including calendar display, cycle estimates, symptom patterns, local insights, and PDF summaries. Local insights may summarize cycle stability, pain trends, flow trends, mood and sleep associations, and PMS-style symptom patterns from the entries saved on your device.

4. Local Storage

Core cycle data is stored on your device. In the current version, MiraCycle does not require a MiraCycle account or upload cycle logs to a MiraCycle server for core tracking.

If you delete the app, iOS may remove local app data according to system behavior. If you back up your device through Apple services, your app data may be included in your device backup depending on your iOS and iCloud settings.

5. Voice Input

If you use voice-assisted entry, MiraCycle asks for microphone and speech recognition permissions. Spoken input is used to create a draft cycle log for your review. You must apply and save the draft before it becomes a calendar entry.

Speech recognition behavior may depend on Apple system services and your device settings. You can manage microphone and speech recognition permissions in iOS Settings.

6. App Lock and Safety Controls

MiraCycle includes optional privacy and safety controls to reduce accidental exposure of sensitive information:

  • Face ID app lock, when enabled by you.
  • Quick Hide, which covers the app behind a private screen.
  • Automatic blur when the app becomes inactive, if enabled.
  • Hidden sensitive fields, which can hide sex/protection, body metrics, and private notes from the daily logging panel.
  • Emergency local data deletion, which removes local cycle data and resets privacy preferences in the app.

Face ID authentication is handled by iOS. MiraCycle does not receive or store your biometric data.

7. PDF Export

PDF reports are generated locally from your saved calendar logs and current export settings. Before sharing a report, review the export privacy switches and the PDF content. After you share or save a PDF using the iOS share sheet, that file is controlled by the destination you choose.

You can choose whether the PDF includes period history, flow, pain, symptoms, mood, discharge, sleep, sex/protection, body metrics, and private notes.

8. Data Sharing

MiraCycle does not require an account and does not intentionally share your cycle logs with third-party advertisers. Data may leave the app only when you choose to export, share, back up, use Apple system speech services, or otherwise transfer it using iOS or another service.

9. Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

The current MiraCycle app is designed without third-party advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, or cross-app tracking. If this changes in a future version, this policy and the App Store privacy details should be updated before release.

10. Children

MiraCycle is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact support.

11. Your Choices

  • You can choose what to record.
  • You can exclude sensitive fields from PDF export.
  • You can hide sensitive logging fields inside the app.
  • You can enable or disable Face ID app lock, Quick Hide, and inactive-app blur.
  • You can manage microphone, speech recognition, and Face ID permissions in iOS Settings.
  • You can delete local data using the app’s emergency clear option when available, or by deleting the app from your device.

12. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as MiraCycle changes. The effective date at the top of this page shows when the policy was last updated.

13. Contact

For support or privacy questions, open an issue at github.com/kevincy/cycleappsupport/issues.

This policy is provided for product transparency and App Store review preparation. It is not legal advice. Consider having a qualified attorney review it before public launch.
